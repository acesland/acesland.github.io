An Amazing Project

AcesLand is everything you could imagine in a drone theme park.

AcesLand will be the premier destination of users, fans and companies of drones. For more than 2 years, we've been undertaking this project. Visitors to AcesLand will can step forward in time to the “Age of Drones”, in the most immersive and comprehensive experience. Design and technology will be discovered for visitors to see the flights, watch the competitions and enchant with the most innovative creations.

The drone park was designed to be explored in an spectacular outdoor setting to feature a world-class amusement park. Learning how futuristic drones might communicate, navigate and operate could aid human development in many areas.

AcesLand brings together knowledge and fun of the world of the drones, in a exciting way unlike anywhere else.

The Quest

The Quest is a GPS-based strategy game.

The Quest is a GPS-based strategy game, so your drones have to move around in the real world to find a secret big treasure, it's an amazing mind challenge for the best pilots, against time and nature.

There’re a lot to the game and its learning curve can be a little intense, but this aventure is unique, a real-world challenge. The Quest encourages you to get out and explore the forest with special drones. In order to play, you have to be physically present at drone park location. In the game, you will have an unlimited range of action to discover points, build maps, link clues and ask help through Internet. That “quest area” is wide, and any point or hidden portal you want to interact with needs to be inside your controlled fields.

As you approach hidden portals, you can “unlock” them for clues, trade to reinforce your team, and link your path to form “controlled fields.” Doing these things earns points for your team, achievements required to level up and access better clues.

There are also “mini quests” which get you extra points and special clues for each completed assignment, that consider find small parts of a treasure in a given place.

Speed Races

The Sport Of Drone Racing.

Drone racing is a sport where drone pilots strive to build extremely agile drones to fly around a set course as fast as possible. Drone races use FPV (First Person View) systems, where pilots use cameras to fly drones as if they were sitting in the cockpit. Some pilots fly using FPV monitors, where as others use specialized FPV goggles to give them a more immersive experience.

Drone racing is already starting in many courses and they're amazing, but it’s just the beginning compared to what drone racing will become in our park. We want to define what it means to be a drone racer in the future.

Museum

A tribute to those visionares who pioneered civilian drone aviation.

The museum will be for the education and enjoyment of all the community. The visitors' experience begins in a classic hangar, where they view movies covering FPV flies in the most stunning landscapes and major events, including historic footage. At the exploration gallery guest will discover why drone and UAS is more than 100 years old, until computer technology evolved and miniaturized equipments in the 1980s and 90s. Hearing from the pioneering developers who built the drone industry and are changing the world.

They will also experience demonstrations of the most advances devices and learn just how powerful a fully operational drone can be, and will explore how technology and drones will impact the way we work, play, and live through the rich selection of onsite and online exhibits.

Drone Academy

Learning To Fly​.

We will host a full Drone Academy in Central Florida with the focus on how to Build, Control, & Program a Drone/Unmanned Aerial Vehicle's (UAV). Each student will start by building your own Multi-Rotor Drone and learn basic flight control on simulators. After that they will be ready to learn how to master their own Drone/UAV with real flight instruction in a controlled area.

Offering in-person training courses, the expert flight team will be led by true aviators with extensive experience. Drone Academy will host hands-on courses for corporate needs, including real estate, professional photography, agriculture, oil and gas exploration, and video production. Drones are being used for public safety, advertising and live streaming. Businesses must do prepared for the explosion of UAV industry and comply with FAA regulations. As many as 100,000 new jobs will be created in the first 10 years after unmanned aircraft are cleared for takeoff in U.S. airspace.

Streaming Events

Up Close With a Live-Streaming Drone.

Camera-equipped drones are everywhere these days. Drone users can transmit high-definition video from the drone to a base station from up to a mile away, then out to viewers around the world in real-time using a service like Ustream...

You can imagine the possibilities – a drone that let people in around the globe watch the Drone Park tournaments live from a bird’s-eye view, complemented by web stats dashboards. But in the near future, online visitors might remotely control drones and be part of sports games, music festivals, and other live events.
